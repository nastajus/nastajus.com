Here is the dynamic Hart Chart web app I created for practicing eye scanning exercises. Please share this with any patients or clinics whom may benefit.

https://nastajus.github.io/hart-chart/   

  

Usage: This is designed to be used on both mobile and desktop, showing at appropriately different font sizes.  A random set of characters is generated, which helps keep the brain more engaged, as the activity can get monotonous and boring over time. To share the identical characters onto another device, use the SHARE button to copy a link and send it to yourself (Email, Facebook Messenger, SMS... etc) to open the identical chart on both devices simultaneously.   

Any feedback please email me the creator at ian.nastajus@gmail.com, It's not perfect yet, so I will try to make improvements over time.

Thanks and enjoy
